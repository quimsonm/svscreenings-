## Filming Instructions

### Setup
- **Location:** Clinic hallway or near ultrasound equipment — reads medical, builds credibility
- **Wear:** Scrubs (biggest trust signal on screen). Badge if available.
- **Camera:** iPhone on tripod or propped stable at eye level — NO handheld
- **Orientation:** Horizontal (landscape)
- **Framing:** Face and chest fill top 2/3 of frame, small amount of headroom
- **Lighting:** Face a window for natural light. Never have light source behind you.
- **Audio:** Use earbuds with inline mic — audio matters more than video quality
- **Quiet room:** No HVAC hum, hallway noise, or echo

### Delivery Tips
- Do 3-5 takes — pick the one where you sound most like yourself
- Slow down — most people rush on camera
- Hook line: slow and serious
- CTA: warm and confident, not urgent or salesy
- Talk to the camera like it's a patient's concerned adult child

## Why Video Beats Static
- Builds parasocial trust in 40 seconds — face + scrubs + clinical setting
- Answers objections inside the ad ("no referral, just show up Saturday")
- Shareable — adult children tag parents, parents send to spouse
- Facebook algorithm rewards video with cheaper reach over time
- Static ad found the audience. Video converts them.
