Drop your images here and they'll be automatically optimized by Astro.

## Format
- Preferred: WebP (best compression)
- Also accepted: JPEG, PNG (Astro will optimize automatically)
- Aim for images under 500KB before optimization

## Style Guide
- Warm, professional healthcare/wellness feel
- Soft, natural lighting preferred
- Avoid harsh clinical/hospital aesthetics
- Diverse, approachable subjects
- Premium/concierge tone — think luxury wellness, not urgent care
